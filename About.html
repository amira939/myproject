About Us

Founded by Amira Mahmoud in 2000 as a tiny Swedish mail-order catalog business, today LOREM IPSUM is a global home furnishing brand that lives in the hearts of many people. Explore the LOREM IPSUM story here, including our heritage, where we are today, and our positive impact on people and the planet. We are a values-driven company with a passion for life at home. Every product we create is our idea for making home a better place. At the LOREM IPSUM Group, we have 315 stores in 27 countries.
